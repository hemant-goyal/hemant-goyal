# 👋 Hi, I'm Hemant Goyal
I'm a biophysicist and researcher from India 🇮🇳 based at PGIMER, Chandigarh.
I bridge the gap between complex biological systems and computational insights through research in multiomics, structural biology, and AMR pathogen diagnostics. I’ve worked on a range of projects, including developing therapeutic peptides as bacterial infections and neural disorders, developing ONT/WGS NGS panels for profiling superbugs, and conducting 16S metagenomics of the human microbiome. My expertise spans from MD simulations n docking to spectroscopy n cellular imaging and in-vivo murine models. When I'm not in the lab, I'm likely gaming rpg or driving my through the mountains.
